Shannon Schultz

Shannon is an artist based in Ann Arbor, Michigan. She has a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree with a major in Photography from the College for Creative Studies. Currently she is working as the Photo Editor for Rivian Automotive and is always looking for new photo and video projects. She loves to focus in portraiture of all kinds, especially action photography and fine art portraiture. Sports have always been a passion of hers and she's always finding new inspiration through athletes of all backgrounds. She loves that action photography not only exposes physical prowess, but it has the ability to express incredible human narratives and beauty.
